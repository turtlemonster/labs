FAQ

Will my dog's feelings get hurt?

Possibly. But at least they'll be in a safe space where everyone understands that their feelings are valid and also slightly ridiculous.

What if my dog is TOO needy?

There's no such thing! That's like saying there's too much ice cream. We celebrate the chaos.

Do you offer virtual therapy?

We tried Zoom sessions, but dogs kept barking at themselves in the screen, which defeated the purpose. Now we're considering it for humans with anxiety about their dogs having Zoom anxiety.

What's included in a typical day?

Breakfast crisis management, morning feelings circle, philosophical walks, snack existentialism, afternoon cuddles, evening emotions debriefing, and the 6pm treat countdown anxiety we all share.
